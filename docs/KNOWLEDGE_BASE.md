## About The AKT Foundation

### Mission
The AKT Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to providing essential household necessities to families in extreme poverty and those impacted by domestic violence.

### How We Help
- Provide household essentials to families in need
- Support domestic violence survivors
- Partner with local shelters and organizations
- 100% of event proceeds go to charitable programs

### Your Support Matters
When you attend The Soup Cook Off, purchase tickets, or become a sponsor, you're directly helping families in our community who are facing difficult circumstances.

### Tax Deductibility
As a registered 501(c)(3), donations to the AKT Foundation may be tax-deductible. Consult your tax advisor for details.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What is The Soup Cook Off?**
A: The Soup Cook Off is a soup tasting festival featuring over 20 chefs preparing their best soups for attendees to taste and vote on. It's a fun, family-friendly event that supports the AKT Foundation.

**Q: Who benefits from this event?**
A: All proceeds benefit the AKT Foundation, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that helps families in extreme poverty and domestic violence survivors.

**Q: Is this event family-friendly?**
A: Yes! The Soup Cook Off is a family-friendly event. Children under 5 are free.

**Q: What should I bring?**
A: Just yourself and an appetite! We provide everything you need including a tasting spoon, voting card, and program. VIP ticket holders receive additional items.

### Ticket Questions

**Q: Can I buy tickets at the door?**
A: Yes, but prices are higher at the gate. We recommend purchasing online to save money and guarantee entry.

**Q: Are tickets refundable?**
A: No, but tickets are transferable. You can give or sell your ticket to someone else.

**Q: What's the difference between GA and VIP?**
A: VIP tickets include early entry (30 minutes before GA), a commemorative soup bowl, reusable grocery bag, and soup tray, in addition to everything GA includes.

**Q: How do I get my tickets?**
A: Tickets purchased more than 10 days before the event ship free. Otherwise, they'll be at will-call, which opens 30 minutes before doors.

### Event Questions

**Q: How long does the event last?**
A: The event runs approximately 4 hours, from 11 AM to 3 PM (VIP entry at 10:30 AM).

**Q: Is parking free?**
A: Parking varies by venue. Check the event page for specific parking information.

**Q: Can I vote for multiple soups?**
A: You receive one voting card to cast your vote for your favorite soup.

**Q: Are there vegetarian/vegan options?**
A: Soup options vary by event. We typically have several non-meat options available.

### Chef Questions

**Q: How do I enter as a chef?**
A: Visit soupcookoff.com/chef-entry, select your division, and complete the registration form.

**Q: What are the chef divisions?**
A: We have Professional, Amateur, and Junior chef divisions.

**Q: What do winners receive?**
A: Winners receive recognition, prizes, and bragging rights! Specific prizes announced before each event.

### Sponsorship Questions

**Q: Why should I sponsor?**
A: Sponsorship provides excellent exposure for your business while supporting a great cause. Packages range from $250 to $2,500.

**Q: Can I customize my sponsorship package?**
A: Yes! We work with sponsors, especially nonprofits, to create packages that fit your needs.

**Q: What's the deadline for sponsorship?**
A: Contact us as early as possible to ensure your sponsorship materials are included in all printed materials.
